    Our Origin

    Lickies was founded in 2023 by a group of ice cream enthusiasts. Starting off as a dream, and then a tiny ice cream stall, lickies has grown into a unique ice cream brand that is enjoyed by many. Spending these last 3 years and counting, lickies has been testing countless recipes, saving the very best for our customers. We even travelled around the globe, collaborating with local suppliers to source out the best ingredients for you and for our Earth.

    Our Purpose

    Our goal is unforgettable ice cream experience that you won't find anywhere else. We believe that ice cream is more than just a dessert - it's an experience that should be savoured and enjoyed with every lick.

    Our Values

    At lickies, we want to stand up for what we believe in. Donating to charities and supporting local communities is a big part of our values. We believe that by giving back, we can make a positive impact on the world around us. We also value sustainability and strive to minimize our environmental footprint by using eco-friendly packaging and sourcing ingredients responsibly.
