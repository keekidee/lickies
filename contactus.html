Contact Us









  • Is your ice-cream halal certified?

    Yes! Our ice cream is halal-certified, so you can enjoy your favourite flavours with peace of mind.

    Do you have dairy free options?

    Unfortunately, we currently do not offer dairy-free ice cream. However, we are always exploring new flavours and options for our customers. If you have an idea, you can send via our feedback form!

    Can I sample the ice cream before ordering?

    Absolutely! We encourage customers to sample our flavours before choosing their perfect scoop.

    Do you only offer delivery in Singapore?

    Yes. Our islandwide delievery is only available in Singapore.

    Can i order ice cream for parties and events?

    Yes! Our ice cream cakes can be customised for special occasions. Please place your order at least 3–5 days in advance.

    How should I store my ice cream?

    Store it in the fridge or freezer! Although our ice cream never melts, it does spoil! Best consumed by 3 days after purchase.

    Do you support any charities?

    Yes! We are proud to say that we support a different charity every 2 months! We have supported many charities like the Speedwagon Foundation, Parade of Hope foundation, Open Path Alliances and many more! We want to support as many charities as we can so we can do our part to make the world a better place.

    Where can we contact you?

    You can reach us through our Contact Us page for questions, feedback, event enquiries or other general enquiries. We will do our best to get back to you as soon as possible.
